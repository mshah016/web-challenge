Weather Across the Globe

In this assignment,
we attempted to extract weather
by latitude. The following graphs
show the data across various cities
in the world. The first graph shows
the temperature distribution alone among
different latitude points. The second graph
shows the humidity distribution among different
latitudes. The third graph shows the distribution of
wind speeds, and the fourth graph shows the distribution
of how cloudy it is at each location. Click on each graph to
see it close up and learn a little bit more about how weather
varies across different latitudes. Use these graphs to create
your own analyses about the locations displayed in these graphs,
and use them to further add to the vastly growing knowledge of
the many environmental properties within each location.
The raw data can be found on the 'Data' tab
to assist you in your analysis. We hope
this work yields many benefits,
and we wish you well.
Good luck!
